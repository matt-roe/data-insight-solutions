# Services

Every organization's data journey is different. We offer a range of services from strategic consulting to hands-on implementation, designed to support you at any stage of your data transformation.

Our approach is collaborative—we work with your team to understand your specific challenges and design solutions that drive real business value.

## Strategic Consulting & Planning

Get the foundation right before making major investments. Our consulting services help you clarify vision, evaluate options, and build a practical roadmap.

- **Macro Process Diagramming** — Visualize your business processes and understand where data flows, where it gets stuck, and where opportunities exist
- **Data & AI Strategy** — Align your data initiatives with business goals; clarify priorities and investment strategies
- **Solution Design & Statement of Work (SoW)** — Design your ideal solution with detailed implementation roadmap and resource requirements
- **Software Product Research** — Evaluate tools and platforms to find the best fit for your needs, constraints, and budget
- **Software Request For Proposal (RFP)** — Develop detailed requirements and evaluation criteria for vendor selection
- **Machine Learning / AI Model Bias & Fairness Analysis** — Ensure your ML/AI initiatives are ethical, unbiased, and produce reliable results
- **AI Consulting** — Explore AI opportunities specific to your business; assess readiness; design responsible AI implementation strategies
- **Integration Design** — Plan how to connect your disparate systems; design data flows, architecture, and governance
- **Business Start-Up Technology Strategy** — Build your technology foundation from the ground up with sustainable, scalable approaches

## Implementation & Administration

From building custom solutions to configuring commercial platforms, we handle the technical work so your team can focus on strategy and operations.

- **Website Design & Development** — Professional websites with custom functionality, content management systems, and integration capabilities
- **Software Onboarding & Configuration** — Implement and configure business systems (VOIP, Email, CRM, ERM, Project Management, and more)
- **Low Code Automation or Integration** — Rapid solutions using no-code and low-code platforms; fast to build and easy for your team to maintain
- **Full Code Automation or Integration** — Custom development for complex workflows, unique business requirements, or system constraints
- **Software Maintenance and Updates** — Ongoing support to keep your systems current, secure, and performing optimally

## Advisory Roles

For organizations needing ongoing technical leadership and strategic guidance, we offer flexible advisory arrangements.

- **Partial CTO** — Chief Technology Officer guidance without full employment; strategic technology planning, architecture decisions, and vendor evaluation
- **Partial CIO** — Chief Information Officer advisory; data strategy, governance, compliance, and organizational technology alignment

## Training & Support

Adoption is everything. We provide training and support to ensure your team gets maximum value from your systems and can evolve them as needs change.

- **Live Training** — Hands-on sessions for your team; custom curriculum based on your tools and processes
- **Technical Support** — Expert guidance when you need it; troubleshooting, optimization, and architectural advice
